Why do we want to discuss these topics?

The UNSC is the most crucial organ of the UN when it comes to crises resolving. PKO and sanctions are the two major means of the SC to regulate Member States from their non-compliance of their obligations, and without these method being efficient and effective, the SC cannot enforce Member States to abide by their obligations. Illicit flows of SALW, on the other hand, causes serious damage to the harmony of the international society, as well as human rights violations due to their mobility and other characteristics; thus, the UN has constantly called for more attention in this area.
