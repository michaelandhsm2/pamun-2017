General Assembly Plenary
Topic A: Eradicating the Mobilization of Child Soldiers

Topic B: Ensuring Decent Livelihoods, Economic Growths and Reduced Inequalities through Partnerships
The United Nations has no vested interest in the status quo. In the dynamic world society which is the objective of the United Nations, all peoples must have equality and equal rights.
— Ralph Bunche, Winner of the 1950 Nobel Peace Prize
議題重要性

Both topics are closely related with human rights protection and the development within Member States.Topic A deals with a relatively intense issue in which we have to alter the current status, while Topic B aims as establishing systems for sustainable development of each countries to ensuring decent livelihood of residents. With these topics discussed in the General Assembly Plenary Session, delegates could utilize more information of past UN actions across different committees of the UN General Assembly.

International Monetary Fund
Topic A: The reform of the IMF: The Moral Hazard Under Asymmetric Information Shared Amongst Nations

Topic B: Combating Governmental Corruptions in Funding of the IMF
Safety and security don't just happen, they are the result of collective consensus and public investment.
— Christine Lagarde, Managing Director of IMF
議題重要性

While IMF seeks to work closely with Member States of the United Nations, it is undeniable that the information shared amongst the States and between the States and IMF is not symmetric due to the existence of moral hazard, leading to the misuses of the fund. Another misuses of the fund could be the corruption of the governments, which is an important topic itself with many aspects other than the asymmetry of information to be discussed.

Organization of Islamic Cooperation
Topic A: Addressing the Conditions Facilitating the Growth of Violent Non-State Actors (VNSAs)

Topic B: Settlement and Reallocation of Syrian and Iraqi Refugees
We meet here not to emphasize unilateral positions or out of the clouds that may envelop our bilateral relations, but to search for a common position that reflects the will of the Ummah (community).
— Iyad Ameen Madani, Secretary General of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation
議題重要性

Violent non-state actors (VNSAs) are major factors of human rights violation, sovereignty invasion, as well as non-compliances or breaches of international law. OIC, as an important role within parts of Asia where various VNSAs exist, is aiming at addressing the conditions leading to the growth of VNSAs. In addition, to assist refugees suffering due to current crises within the region and to protect their human rights are also the goal of the OIC.

Security Council
Topic A: Measures to Enhance Enforcement Actions of the Security Council in Restoring International Peace and Security

Topic B: Consolidating Communications and Adapting to Newly Developed Technology in Combating Illicit Trades of Small Arms and Light Weapons (SALW)
Safety and security don't just happen, they are the result of collective consensus and public investment.
— Nelson Mandel
議題重要性

The UNSC is the most crucial organ of the UN when it comes to crises resolving. PKO and sanctions are the two major means of the SC to regulate Member States from their non-compliance of their obligations, and without these method being efficient and effective, the SC cannot enforce Member States to abide by their obligations. Illicit flows of SALW, on the other hand, causes serious damage to the harmony of the international society, as well as human rights violations due to their mobility and other characteristics; thus, the UN has constantly called for more attention in this area.

ICMW台灣施行研討會
議題：落實保護移工國際公約對來台外籍家事類勞工之人權與保障
想要在一朝一夕、甚至是一年之內促成改變，對任何人來說都不是件容易事；但是只要那樣的政治意志確實存在，就一定會有改變的可能。
— Sima Samar 兩次台灣公政公約審查委員

在全球化的浪潮下，保護外國移工的權利成為越來越重要的議題。台灣對於外國移工的需求帶來了新的社會變遷，增加了我們對<保護所有移工及其家庭成員權利國際公約>探討的需要，並促成未來於台灣有更完整的實施。
