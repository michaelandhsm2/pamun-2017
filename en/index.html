The Discussion

Diverse Topics Areas

PAMUN has always been known for its innovative and diverse areas of discussion. With topics ranging from Human Rights and Country Development to Geopolitical Conflict and International Law, you can be rest assured that there is a committee in PAMUN that will intrigue you.

Intriguing Discussions

With participants coming from various countries and diverse backgrounds, Pan Asia MUN provides delegates with an opportunity to debate and share their different point of views in an empowering and engaging environment.

Academic Excellence

Pan Asia MUN is planned with both first time participants and experienced MUNers in mind. Not only will we provide various infographics and preparation guides to ease first time participants into the MUN community, our experienced academic team also has various surprises planned for experienced participants.
